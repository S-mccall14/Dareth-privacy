Privacy Policy for Truth or Dare App

Effective Date: January 19, 2026

1. Data Collection

This app collects player names only to run the current game session. Names are used locally on the device and are not stored, shared, or transmitted to any external servers.

2. Family Mode

Family Mode is designed for general audiences playing together. Users are encouraged to use nicknames instead of real names. No personal data is retained after the session ends.

3. Consent

By entering a name or nickname, users consent to its use during the current game session only. Users may revoke consent at any time by restarting or closing the app.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or payment services. No data is shared with third parties.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: truthordareapp@gmail.com
